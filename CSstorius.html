Project Overview

Date: Dec 2020 - March 2021
Tool: Figma
Position: Researcher, Product Designer

Storius is a mobile application for providing travelers with local insights in short audio clips that are location-based and crowd-sourced. In summary, Storius aims to be a platform for travelers that want to explore new places through stories, which function like when you listen to podcasts along the way to work. The stories vary to different styles, including educational history, folklore, or personal experience of content providers. To make this project works, we rely on 2 parties: listeners and content providers. For this case study, we will be focusing on the listener side.
