Project Overview

Date: November 2021
Tool: Figma
Position: Researcher, Visual Designer, Product Designer

This is the final project of my Design Thinking class. We have around 2 months to work on and deliver a high-fidelity prototype as a final product. We will be judged by the FRUIT model: financial viability, rarity, usefulness & desirability, impact, and team effort & learning, which has been used by many Venture Capital to consider the sustainability of startups when funding.


We received some requirement according to the course

  • use the Design Thinking tools when solving the problem
  • Our solution must aim to achieve the 17 GOALS of Sustainable development Growth by the United Nations. It is designed to be a "blueprint for achieving a better and more sustainable future for all."
