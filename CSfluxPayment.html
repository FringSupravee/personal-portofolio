Project Overview

Date: July - Setember 2021
Tool: Figma
Position: Project Manager, Head of Marketing

Flux payments is a hong kong-based financial technology startup creating a platform to reward good financial behavior. The startup has been part of Cyberport University and the Cyberport Creative Micro Fund Grant (CCMF) winner in 2021. The mission of Flux Payments is to help the Hong Kong population transition into a financially disciplined lifestyle.



Outcomes


1/5 Research Insights

Hong Kong consumers spend a significant proportion of their incomes on repayments like credit card utilities. However, 33% of Hong Konger face difficulties in managing them due to laziness and being unaware of their service contracts.
According to SCMP (2017), Hong Kong credit card holders are least aware of their credit score if they just pay the minimum. They also struggle with keeping track of what they owe regularly and fail to pay their personal loans and credit card debt on time.

Focus group interview

As a designer, articles from the news are helpful. However, I was curious about 'why' and the reasons behind our target users' actions. So I arranged the focus group to interview people with different age ranges and professional backgrounds. Here is the result that we have received.

  • 92% of respondents mentioned laziness/ forgetting being the reason behind their late payment.
  • 66% of respondents admitted to defaulting on their payment.
  • 76% of respondents pay at least 30% of their expenses with credit.
  • 97% of respondents are willing to pay bills early if incentivized.


2/5 Define Problem

HMW Statement

How might we design a trusting, incentivized, and better experience for everyone involved in the bill payment process.



3/5 Ideate

Core Feature

Throughout the Ideate process, I have engage mission of the company which are.

  • Consilidates all bill payments.
  • remind user to pay bill and have healthy relationship with finance.
  • Rewards early payment with Flux point*, which can be redeemed across partner ecosystem.

*Flux point is the reward point that customers will receive if they pay the bills on times. the amount of Flux point that customers will get depends on amount of spending money and how early they pay the bills.


Hence, I have discussed with developers and stakeholders which we have came up with these 3 features.

  1. Customer onboarding.
  2. Coupon redemption from Flux point.
  3. Reminder and tracker for all of the up-coming bills.

5/5 Conclusion

Final feature of the application

Onboarding customers
  • Design fast onboarding to register an account to create an account.
  • Make customer support chat easy to access.
  • Inform the user about the upcoming steps.

Monthly bills' tracker and reminder
  • Visualize the monthly spending into charts, which customers can compare each month's spending.
  • Provide detail on each bill before making payment.
  • Allow customers to select/deselect the accounts they want to pay at the current stage.
  • Notify once receiving the bills and dates that they need to pay.

Coupon redemption
  • Add the distance and navigation of its nearest shop to the users.
  • Separate coupons and stores into different categories.
  • Use AI to promote the ads and articles that align with the user's preference according to history search and spending.
  • Allow users to 'save' the coupon for later users.
  • Shorten the process by just scrolling and scanning the QR code when claiming a coupon.
